Demonstration of Tree Structure Pseudo Classes

CSS pseudo-classes related to tree structure allow you to select elements based on their position within the document tree, specifically in relation to their siblings or parents. They are very useful for styling lists, tables, or any structured content without adding extra classes or IDs.
